The five problems we solve

Your compliance team
can't listen to every call. We can.

"CMS will fine us if agents miss a disclosure"

CMS requires agents to record all sales and enrollment calls in their entirety. SOAs must be obtained 48 hours in advance and retained for 10 years.4 CMS uses secret shoppers to test compliance.3 In the first four months of 2025, CMS fined Medicare plans more money than in the previous four years combined - over $3 million.1 Violations mean civil monetary penalties, suspension of marketing, or termination of your ability to sell Medicare plans entirely. And your QA team is sampling 2-5% of calls.

How Attention solves this

100% call coverage. Every call scored against compliance scorecards that track CMS-required disclosures, SOA language, and recording consent. "High compliance risk" labels flag violations instantly with timestamps - was the disclosure made, exactly when in the call. Your QA team reviews flagged exceptions, not random 2% samples.

"Coaching doesn't scale during enrollment season"

AEP is 54 days.6 Agents work 7-day weeks. Managers responsible for 10 agents suddenly have 25-30 as seasonal hires ramp up. Manual coaching drops to zero - there's no time. New agents hired for enrollment get almost no guidance. 90% of new agents fail in their first year.2 It costs agencies $50,000-$200,000 and three years to bring a producer to validation.7 Every washout is a six-figure loss.

How Attention solves this

AI coaching runs on every call, 365 days a year - including AEP, OEP, and the seasonal hiring surge. Every agent scored. Managers see dashboards and exception reports, not individual call reviews. Legacy Family Life's target: double or triple their agent-to-manager ratio from 10:1 to 25:1.

"Sales dispositions take 45 minutes per call"

After every enrollment, agents enter policy details, product selections, pricing, payment info, and application data into the CRM. This process averages 45 minutes per call when done manually. During AEP, when an agent might handle 15-30+ calls a day, the math doesn't work. Agents choose between taking the next call and finishing their paperwork. The paperwork loses. Every time.

How Attention solves this

Auto sales disposition. Attention listens to the call, extracts all relevant data - products, pricing, health conditions, payment details - fills out the application, submits it, and emails the agent anything that might have been missed. Legacy Family Life went from 45 minutes to 1-2 minutes.

"Our agents' client lists are worth tens of millions"

In life and health insurance, a book of business is worth tens of millions in lifetime commissions. Agents are financially motivated to extract client lists before they leave. As one agency owner told us: "The money on the table, if an agent is trying to get all our contact info, is large enough that they would be trying to get around it." If your AI tools give agents access to raw client data, you've built a self-serve extraction tool.

How Attention solves this

PII protection with role-based permissions. Agents use AI coaching and call analysis without accessing raw client data. Granular controls prevent data extraction. As one customer put it: "The last thing I want them to do is pull a list of all our clients."

"We can't detect fraud until it's too late"

Fraudulent applications - agents misrepresenting client health conditions, income, or eligibility - cost agencies carrier contracts and create legal liability. Misrepresentation accounts for 25% of all claims against life and health agents.8 By the time fraud surfaces during a claim review, the carrier relationship is already at risk. One bad enrollment can cascade.

How Attention solves this

Fraud detection scoring. Attention reads the medical underwriting section of each call, compares what the client said to what the agent submitted, and generates a fraud likelihood percentage. Flagged calls go to management immediately - not months later during a claim.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know.

Is Attention HIPAA compliant?

Yes. Attention is HIPAA compliant with a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) in place. We serve Medicare and health insurance teams including Elite Insurance Partners, Legacy Family Life, and Highland Health Care, and meet all HIPAA requirements for protected health information.

Is call recording compliant in two-party consent states?

Yes. Attention supports call recording compliance across all 50 states, including two-party consent jurisdictions. Recording disclosure prompts are built into the workflow.

How does Attention help with E&O protection?

The average E&O claim costs $40,000+ and raises your premiums 25-50% for three to five years. The #1 defense is documented proof that the agent discussed the coverage and gave appropriate advice. Attention creates that documentation automatically - a timestamped summary of every call showing what was discussed, what advice was given, and what the client decided. Stored in compliance with state retention requirements of 5-10+ years.

Can Attention handle AEP volume?

Yes. Elite Insurance Partners processes 30,000+ calls per day through Attention. The platform is built for the volume spikes that come with AEP (Oct 15 - Dec 7), OEP, and special enrollment periods. Compliance scoring, coaching, and auto-disposition run at the same speed whether you're processing 100 calls or 30,000.

How long does setup take?

Agents are live in minutes. Attention connects to your existing dialer (Vonage, Aircall, ONYX) and video tools (Zoom, Teams, Meet). Compliance scorecards and coaching workflows are configured during onboarding with hands-on support from our deployment team - we build the prompts and scorecards with you, not hand you a manual.

Does CMS require call recordings to be stored for 10 years?

Yes. CMS requires retention of all Medicare sales call recordings for 10 years, and SOA forms must also be retained for 10 years regardless of whether a plan was sold.4 Attention stores call recordings, transcripts, compliance scores, and AI summaries in a HIPAA-compliant environment with BAA in place. All data is accessible for audits at any point during the retention period.
