Coaching doesn't scale - especially during AEP

AI coaching runs on every call, 365 days a year - including AEP, OEP, and the seasonal hiring surge. Every agent scored on script adherence and required disclosures. Every call is transcribed, summarized, and pushed into your CRM and dialer automatically. Full context lives in your systems, not the agent's notebook. Any rep can pick up any client relationship without missing a beat. PII controls ensure agents can't extract client lists on the way out.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know.

Is Attention HIPAA compliant?

Yes. Attention is HIPAA compliant with a Business Associate Agreement (BAA) in place. We serve Medicare and health insurance teams including Elite Insurance Partners, Legacy Family Life, and Highland Health Care, and meet all HIPAA requirements for protected health information.

What dialers does Attention integrate with?

Essentially any dialer. Vonage, Aircall, ONYX, ONYX, and more. If your agents are dialing from it, Attention can plug in. No hardware required, no changes to agent workflows. We also push data back into your dialer and CRM so everything stays in sync.

How does Attention help with E&O protection?

The average E&O claim costs $40,000+ and raises your premiums 25-50% for three to five years. The #1 defense is documented proof that the agent discussed the coverage and gave appropriate advice. Attention creates that documentation automatically - a timestamped summary of every call showing what was discussed, what advice was given, and what the client decided. Stored in compliance with state retention requirements of 5-10+ years.

Can Attention handle AEP volume?

Yes. Elite Insurance Partners processes 30,000+ calls per day through Attention. The platform is built for the volume spikes that come with AEP (Oct 15 - Dec 7), OEP, and special enrollment periods. Compliance scoring, coaching, and auto-disposition run at the same speed whether you're processing 100 calls or 30,000.

How long does setup take?

Agents are live in minutes. Attention connects to whatever dialer you're running - Vonage, Aircall, ONYX, ONYX, and more. Compliance scorecards and coaching workflows are configured during onboarding with hands-on support from our deployment team - we build the prompts and scorecards with you, not hand you a manual.

Does CMS require call recordings to be stored for 10 years?

Yes. CMS requires retention of all Medicare sales call recordings for 10 years, and SOA forms must also be retained for 10 years regardless of whether a plan was sold.4 Attention stores call recordings, transcripts, compliance scores, and AI summaries in a HIPAA-compliant environment with BAA in place. All data is accessible for audits at any point during the retention period.
