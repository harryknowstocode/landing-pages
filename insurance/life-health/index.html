The five problems we solve

Sound familiar?
We built for this.

"CMS will fine us if agents miss a disclosure"

Medicare sales are regulated by CMS. Agents must deliver specific disclosures on every call - scope of appointment, recording consent, plan comparison disclaimers. Compliance teams manually sample 2-5% of calls. The rest go unmonitored. CMS audits are real, and violations mean fines, suspension, or loss of carrier contracts.

How Attention solves this

100% call coverage. Every call scored against compliance scorecards. "High compliance risk" labels flag violations instantly. Timestamped documentation for every disclosure - was it said, when was it said. Your QA team reviews exceptions, not random samples.

"Coaching doesn't scale during enrollment season"

During AEP and OEP, call volumes spike. Managers responsible for 10 agents suddenly have 25-30. Manual coaching drops to zero. New agents hired for enrollment season get almost no coaching at all. New producer failure rates are 70-80%.

How Attention solves this

AI coaching runs 365 days a year, including peak enrollment. Every agent scored on every call. Managers see dashboards, not individual call reviews. Legacy Family Life's goal: double or triple their rep-to-manager ratio - from 10:1 to 25:1.

"Sales dispositions take 45 minutes per call"

After every enrollment, agents enter policy details, product selections, pricing, payment info, and application data into the CRM. This process averages 45 minutes per call. During peak enrollment, agents choose between taking the next call and finishing their paperwork.

How Attention solves this

Auto sales disposition. Attention listens to the call, extracts all relevant data - products, pricing, health conditions, payment details - fills out the application, submits it, and emails the agent anything that might have been missed. Legacy Family Life went from 45 minutes to 1-2 minutes.

"Our agents' client lists are worth tens of millions"

In life and health insurance, a book of business is extremely valuable. Agents who leave often try to take client contact information. In large agencies, this represents tens of millions in lifetime commissions. If agents can query CRM data through AI tools, they can extract client lists.

How Attention solves this

PII protection with role-based permissions. Agents use AI coaching and call analysis without accessing raw client data. Granular controls prevent data extraction. As one customer put it: "The last thing I want them to do is pull a list of all our clients."

"We can't detect fraud until it's too late"

Fraudulent applications - where agents misrepresent client health conditions, income, or eligibility - cost agencies carrier contracts and create legal liability. By the time fraud is caught during a claim, the damage is done.

How Attention solves this

Fraud detection scoring. Attention reads the medical underwriting section of each call, compares what the client said to what the agent submitted, and generates a fraud likelihood percentage. Flagged calls go to management immediately - not months later during a claim.
